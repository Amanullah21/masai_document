## **1. Navigating to the Course Template Section**  
1. Click on the **Course** tab to access the **Module** section.  
2. Click the **Create and Manage Template** button.  

<img width="937" alt="Create template" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/6b153851-ff45-4197-b9c0-b35c5309d139" />


## **2. Creating a New Course Template**  
1. Fill in the course template details:  
   - **Title**  
   - **Display Name**  
   - **Description**  
   - **Duration**  
2. Click the **Save Template** button.  

<img width="959" alt="Save template" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/8f4fe980-383d-464a-9118-4f58d47a2c29" />


## **3. Adding Content to the Course Template**  
1. Click the **Select Content** button.

<img width="911" alt="select content" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/8820fbf5-9981-4e5c-802a-fff537d5319b" />


   <p><a href="/Readme.md">Publish Content</a></p>
   
3.Select a **Content Type**:  
   - **Assessment** (Select required resources)  
   - **Video**  
   - **Document**  
   - **Session/Lecture**  
   - **AI Assessment**  



<img width="584" alt="select content type1" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/e1803d2f-97ef-4dd1-9efc-acf706ba29fe" />


## **4. Configuring Content Details**  
1. Enter the following content details:  
   - **Start Date**  
   - **End Date**  
   - **Scheduled Time**  
   - **Conclude Time**  
2. Click the **Save Course Content** button.
